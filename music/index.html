Pablo Discobar
Soundsystem

Pablo Discobar Soundsystem presents the nice side of music, mixing really old shit with a modern sound of disco and house into a uplifting blend. Curious of how it all started this guys dig deep so updating their musical library is a day to day activity. Always on the road for music and gigs, now also developing their own sound by spending days and nights in the studio. They play big venues like Thuishaven, Soenda, Welcome to the future, Amsterdam open air and many more.

Kraak &
Smaak(DJ)

Dutch producers, DJ’s and live act Kraak & Smaak have built up a jaw-dropping track record since their first EP dropped in the early 00s: five studio albums, four remix albums and a long string of singles and remixes on labels such as Jalapeno Records, Future Disco and Spinnin’. Their original sound, consisting of a blend of disco, funk, electronic and house music, has also brought their dj sets and the live band to all corners of the world and on festivals such as Glastonbury, SXSW and Coachella. At this moment the trio is working on their 6th studio album and preparing another world tour for 2019.

Philou
Louzolo

With electronic captured in an African soul, he’s making a name for himself in Holland and far beyond. Louzolo’s signature sound is also his message: representing the pan-african culture and voice in the electronic music arena. He excels in an effortless musical marriage of electronic music and traditional music from the continent that carries his roots. Philou is aware about socially-political issues within the industry and keen to step into the break for his ideals.

Oliver ‘N Criss
& Astrid Kunst

Olivier de Ronde Bresser and Crispijn Berendsen have been playing together as Oliver 'N Criss for 5 years. Their genre is deep house disco. Oliver 'N Criss are nowadays in big clubs and at festivals throughout the Netherlands and abroad. From parties such as Happy Feelings in Amsterdam, Groningen, Utrecht and Rotterdam to large festivals in Greece and London. Festivals such as Wonderland Festival at Thuishaven to their own parties from Warehouse in Delft and Scheveningen.

Zwarte
Koffie

Meet Zwarte koffie! In addition to the regular evenings in the Paradiso, TivoliVredenburg and Lowlands, the rest of the country is now becoming familiar with this extra strong recipe. In the famous Zwarte Koffie style, all corners of the musical spectrum are visited. For example, a typical evening of Zwarte Koffie leads from Hiphop to House, and from Disco to new electronic tracks. All this under the guidance of DJs D-Rok and MR.Speak, who show a strong mix of black music in its broadest form. Get ready for an extra strong coffee.

Kremlin
Disko

The man behind Kremlin Disko is Antonio Pairel. His roots lie in Russia, what might explain his favorite drink: vodka. Music has always been his drive. Big chance he inherited his taste for music from his father, who has a passion for music as well. Raised with music from artists as Stevie Wonder, Earth Wind & Fire and George Benson, the foundation for his music inspiration has been built well. We are convinced – are you ready to do some groovy moves?

Valvetronic
Brassband

Valvetronic Brassband, from Amsterdam is focusing on House/Dance/HipHop. This group of horn players is the best festival act you could wish for. With ten horns, percussion, drums and a lot of enthusiasm Valvetronic Brassband will get the crowd dancing anytime. Inspired by bands like Gallowstreet, No BS Brassband the boys decided to start their own brass band with danceable music. The band is composing their own music with a lot of energy, a lot of climaxes and a lot of jumping parts. You have to experience this explosion of beats, brass and bangs live!!

Moods

Announcing himself with a style that owed much to the soulful producers like Pete Rock, J Dilla, and Nujabes, Moods’ early beats quickly began gaining traction online and started building an impressive following after winning favour with several key tastemaker blogs. Before long he attracted the attention of Dutch dance music royalty – Kraak & Smaak and they requested he remix their track ‘Don’t Let People’ on their 2013 release ‘Chrome Waves Remixed’. The mix he turned in impressed them so much he became the first signing to their newly formed imprint ‘Boogie Angst’.

We Are
Hunters

The hunting collective We Are Hunters from Nijmegen has broken down many venues in recent years with their sharp mix of Classic Rock, the best from the skate parks of the 00's and a healthy touch of pop. With the win of the Roos van Nijmegen, a packed Grenswerkkerk during the Summer Park parties and a sold-out release in Luxor live in the pocket, the five are ready for the next hunting season. But don't worry - No animals were harmed during the making of Rock ’n’ Roll

Stedelijk Gedoe
(Hugo Out)

"Stedelijk Gedoe" derives his name from his love for funky 70’s, 80’s and 90’s urban music, and combines it with contemporary styles. Preferably on vinyl, his genres range from soul, funk, R&B and Hip-Hop to House and Nu-Disco. His goal is to not be limited to one genre, but to create a vibe specifically made for the event he plays at. He tries to combine old-school with new-school music to enlighten it’s listeners with surprising combinations of music, to chill and for dancing. He has made mixes for Rewind Amsterdam and Operator Radio Rotterdam.

This duo knows exactly how to get the people dancing and forget all about time.
